Description

Use this quick and easy recipe to make a hearty, homemade breakfast all on your own!

  • Refrigerated Biscuit
  • Sausage
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Seasonings
  1. Prepare the biscuits
  2. Cook the sausage
  3. Add the flour, stir in milk and bring to boil
  4. Reduce to a simmer and continue cooking for 2 min
  5. Season to taste
