Description

This spaghetti is simple to make, and will taste like a real gourmet dish

  • Spaghetti
  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese, Cottage Cheese, Sour Cream
  • Cheese
  1. Boil the spaghetti in water
  2. Cook beef on stove, transfer to bowl with spaghetti sauce
  3. Prepare baking dish, spread half of the noodles in the bottom
  4. Top with mixture of cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream
  5. Cover with remaining spaghetti in butter
  6. Pour beef mix over spaghetti and spread evenly
  7. Bake for 30 mins, top with cheese and melt
