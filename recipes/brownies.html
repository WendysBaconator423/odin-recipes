Description

These brownies are quick and easy to make and will leave you wanting more!

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Walnuts
  1. Add all ingredients besides walnuts into bowl
  2. Mix, then add walnuts after mixed
  3. Spread mixed batter into brownie
  4. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry
  5. Cool, then cut
